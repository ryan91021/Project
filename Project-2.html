Introduction

This project presents an approach that integrates Gradient Descent and Newton's Method to enhance the probability of achieving the optimal solution. This integration mitigates the slow convergence of Gradient Descent and the initial value problem of Newton's Method, resulting in faster and more reliable optimization.

Performance

In general, Gradient Descent is easier to use for finding the optimal solution in high-dimensional spaces, but it converges slowly. Newton's Method, on the other hand, can converge quadratically but is very sensitive to the initial value. This hybrid method addresses the drawbacks of both methods while leveraging their advantages.
